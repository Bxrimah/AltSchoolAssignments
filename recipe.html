Recipe for Egusi Soup

This is a delicious Nigerian soup made with ground melon seeds, vegetables, and spices.


Recipe Information

  • Preparation Time: 30 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 1 hour
  • Number of Servings: 4
  • Difficulty Level: Easy

Recipe Description

Ingredients

  • 2 cups ground melon seeds (egusi)
  • 1 cup spinach (or any leafy vegetable)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, blended
  • 1-2 Scotch bonnet peppers (to taste)
  • 4 cups water (or as needed)
  • Salt and seasoning cubes (to taste)

Instructions

  1. In a pot, heat some oil and saut√© the chopped onions until translucent.
  2. Add the blended tomatoes and Scotch bonnet peppers, and cook until the mixture thickens.
  3. Stir in the ground melon seeds and mix well.
  4. Add water gradually, stirring to avoid lumps, until you achieve your desired consistency.
  5. Season with salt and seasoning cubes, and let it simmer for about 30 minutes.
  6. Finally, add the spinach and cook for an additional 5-10 minutes until the spinach is tender.
  7. Serve hot with pounded yam, rice, or any preferred side dish.

Notes

Feel free to adjust the spice level by adding more or fewer Scotch bonnet peppers. You can also add proteins like fish or meat for a heartier meal.
